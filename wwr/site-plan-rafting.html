Purpose

Heart-pounding rapids and gentle, soothing flows are just the vehicle for your transport into a world beyond anything familiar. The 2,000-foot-high red rock canyon walls reveal a fresh, amazing view with every twist and turn.

Audience

The Green River offers something fun for all family members. The river runs hundreds of miles from the Wind River Mountains in Wyoming to its confluence with the Colorado River in Canyonlands National Park.
